Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I schedule my first/new session?

A: Your coach will send you an email to schedule your first session.

Q: How do I change/cancel a session?

A: Send an email to your coach to reschedule or cancel a session. Be advised that you may incur a session charge if you don’t inform your coach within a sufficient amount of time. Generally, a 24-hour advanced notice is required. Please check with your organization’s appointee for confirmation of the cancellation/rescheduling policy in place.

Q: How will I know when the time I requested through for my coaching session has been confirmed by my coach?

A: Through TimeTrade Scheduling, you will receive an email at the address you provided while scheduling your appointment that will confirm the time and date you selected.

Q: How do I change my time zone?

A: Located on the top right hand corner of your Dashboard is the Time Zone button. Click on it to change your time zone.

Q: How are the Focus Area and Success Metrics determined?

A: The Focus Area and Success Metrics are determined by one of two processes. An organization may choose to provide this information as part of the coaching engagement which will be prepopulated into your Dashboard. The alternative is the coach and coachee may determine the overall focus and agree on the metrics for measuring success. When the Focus Area and Success Metrics have been agreed upon, your coach will update this in your Coachee Profile.

Q: Who creates, edits and is able to view the Progress Notes?

A: Progress Notes are used when your organization wants to track the progress of coaching against Focus Areas and/or Success Metrics. If your organization is not tracking these, you will not use this section. You and your coach are the only two who will be able to see the Progress Notes until you approve them. You may discuss with your coach to make appropriate changes until both parties agree with the language. Once approved, your organization’s appointee will be able to see only the approved Progress Notes.

Q: What if I don’t approve of the Progress Notes?

A: The Progress Notes section is designed as a tool. Discuss it with your coach and/or company’s appointee if you disagree with the notes. If you are unable to come to an agreement, you are not required to approve them. Until approved, the sponsor will not be able to see the progress notes for that session.

Q: Who creates, edits and is able to view the Personal Notes?

A: Personal Notes are used as a communication tool between you and your coach only. You and your coach are the only two who will be able to create, edit and view these notes.

Q: How do I fill out a requested document and add it back to my Dashboard?

A: Click on the document you would like to fill in. In the Document Management pop-up, click on DOWNLOAD. Download the document to your computer. Fill it out as requested and save it. Go back into the Dashboard and click on the document. In the Document Management pop-up, click on CHOOSE and select your document from your computer. Next, click on Open and your document will upload to your Dashboard.
